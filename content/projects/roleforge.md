* Built RoleForge, a local-first FastAPI platform that turns automation-discovered job postings into tailored resume and cover letter packets with SQLite-backed tracking and filesystem-based artifact storage.
* Engineered a staged AI tailoring pipeline with reusable candidate-context artifacts, two-model inference, semantic verification, ATS checks, and PDF packet generation to keep outputs both efficient and evidence-grounded.
* Developed a review-centered workflow spanning job inbox ingestion, live run event tracing, asynchronous queue execution, and application management, turning an LLM feature into an end-to-end operational product.
