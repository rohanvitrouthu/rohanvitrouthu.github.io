* Built a sector-based stock analysis and forecasting project on NIFTY 50 data, organizing 50 equities across 14 sectors and creating merged datasets for cross-sector and sector-level research.
* Developed exploratory analytics and predictive modeling workflows using correlation analysis, ARIMA, regression, Random Forest, and LSTM/RNN approaches to study sector behavior and forecast stock trends.
* Designed the project as a foundation for future refactoring into a production-grade investment research pipeline with reproducible data engineering, walk-forward validation, and portfolio decision support.
