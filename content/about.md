### My Journey

I am a Software and Data Engineer passionate about bridging the gap between raw data and actionable intelligence. With over two years of professional experience, my career has been dedicated to designing scalable data architectures, developing robust ETL pipelines, and building high-performance backend APIs.

My journey began in Hyderabad, India, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology while working as an Informatica ETL Developer for PepsiCo. There, I led complex cloud migrations and engineered pipelines managing terabytes of daily inventory data, laying a solid foundation in enterprise data reliability.

To deepen my expertise, I pursued a Master of Science in Data Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). This advanced study enabled me to integrate modern AI/ML workflows and cutting-edge cloud infrastructure into my engineering toolkit.

Today, as a Software Engineer specialized in Data Platforms at The Home Depot, I architect end-to-end serverless data platforms leveraging Medallion architectures and advanced agentic AI orchestrations. Whether I'm deploying models on serverless inference gateways or orchestrating data via Delta Lake, I thrive on collaborative problem-solving to deliver resilient, data-driven enterprise applications. 

Check out my detailed work history in the [Experience](/experience/) section or view the diverse range of technical solutions I've built under [Projects](/projects/).
